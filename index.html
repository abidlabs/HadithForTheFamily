June 16
~ GENTLENESS ~
I served the Prophet (ﷺ) for ten years, and he never said “Uff” to me, nor did he ever blame me by saying, “Why did you do so or why didn't you do so?” ― Anas
SAHIH MUSLIM
Apply it with your family! Be positive around your family and friends. If they do something that you do not like, then find a positive or humorous way to let them know instead of complaining. Cultivate gratitude. Take a moment right now to think about the positive qualities of the people around you. Verbally show your family love and appreciation on a regular basis.
About

The Hadith are sayings attributed to the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) that teach us how to live good lives in accordance to the will of God.

Hadith for the Family is a project created by Abubakar Abid and the Abid Family. Each day, we select one Hadith, and suggest a practical way to apply the Hadith with your family. Our project is inspired by a number of other beautiful projects like Hadith.fyi and Ayah a Day.

May Allah accept from us and guide us all.
